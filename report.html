Implementation Roadmap

How to action this report.

This is the order in which to work through your findings. Start with the highest-impact fixes that cost nothing to implement. Build from there.

```
Phase 1 · This Week
Zero cost. Maximum impact.

1. Turn on Instant Book if it is currently off — this is the single highest-leverage free action available to most operators. The Airbnb algorithm suppresses non-instant-book listings in search. You will see impression uplift within 48 hours.

2. Review your minimum stay settings and identify any orphan gap nights in your calendar this month. Manually drop the minimum stay to 1 night for any gap of 1 night sitting between bookings. Price that night at 85% of your standard ADR to ensure fill.

3. Check your last 10 reviews. Look for any recurring language — guests mentioning the same thing twice is a signal. Fix any maintenance or experience issue flagged in reviews before your next guest arrives.

Phase 2 · This Month
Structural fixes. Build the engine.

4. Build your pre-arrival message sequence. Send a message 3 days before check-in with an early check-in offer (£25-40), a local recommendations guide, and a direct ask for a 5-star review at checkout. This alone recovers £30-80 per booking in missed upsell revenue.

5. Audit your pricing calendar for the next 90 days. Identify your shoulder weeks — the weeks either side of your peak periods — and adjust minimum stays and pricing specifically for those windows. Shoulder season is where most operators leave the most money.

6. Recalculate your true net RevPAR. Take your gross monthly revenue, subtract platform commission (typically 14-17% on Airbnb), subtract cleaning costs, subtract any management fees. The number left is what your property actually earns. Compare it to your ADR and occupancy. Most operators are surprised by how far apart these numbers are.

Phase 3 · Next 60–90 Days
Strategic. Compounding returns.

7. Start building your direct booking channel. This does not mean building a full website immediately. Start by collecting guest email addresses post-stay and creating a simple direct booking offer for return guests — even a 5% discount vs Airbnb pricing saves you the 14-17% commission. Net result: you earn more, guest pays less, platform takes nothing.

8. Analyse your competitive set. Search for properties of similar type, size and location to yours on Airbnb. Look at the top 5 performers. What do their photos show that yours don't? What do their titles say? What do their reviews focus on? This is free market research. Most operators never do it.

9. Re-run this audit in 90 days. The point of a forensic audit is not a one-time snapshot — it is a baseline. Once you have implemented the Phase 1 and 2 actions, your numbers will have moved. A second audit will show you what shifted, what improved, and where the next layer of opportunity sits.
```
